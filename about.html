CNN Worldwide is the most honored brand in cable news, reaching more individuals on television and online than any other cable news organization in the United States. Globally, people across the world can watch CNN International, which is widely distributed in over 200 countries and territories. CNN Digital is the #1 online news destination, with more unique visitors than any other news source.

CNN’s award-winning portfolio includes non-scripted programming from CNN Original Series and CNN Films for broadcast, streaming and distribution across multiple platforms. CNN programming can be found on CNN, CNN International and CNN en Español channels, on the CNN Originals hub on discovery+, on Max and for pay TV subscription via CNN.com, CNN apps, and cable operator platforms. Additionally, CNN Newsource is the world’s most extensively utilized news service, partnering with over 1,000 local and international news organizations around the world. CNN AI Guiding Principles

We are committed to the utmost journalistic integrity and accuracy

Any use of AI – generative or otherwise – will adhere to CNN’s values and will not undermine the trust audiences have in our journalism. Accuracy and fair representation will remain our top priority. We will collaborate with organizations that have similar AI ethical values.

We are innovators and experimenters

We are a company with a legacy of using new technologies to advance our journalism. We will invest in AI technology to improve user experience, boost productivity, explore new creative opportunities, and combat misinformation and disinformation.

We have human oversight and guardrails

Our employees have oversight and responsibility for the AI systems and tools used, and the content they produce. We undertake rigorous due diligence to evaluate our tools and partners to ensure adherence to CNN and Warner Bros. Discovery’s AI principles.

We are transparent

We clearly signify to our users and audiences when they are seeing, hearing, or reading AI content.

We are committed to fair representation and diversity

We respect diversity of every kind when building or utilizing innovative technologies such as AI tools and services.

We believe the content created by our world-class journalists is unparalleled and valuable

AI presents new opportunities to both protect the intellectual property of our content and commercially support our business.
